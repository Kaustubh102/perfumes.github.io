about us

what makes our perfume special?

Our Perfumes are made when a carefully created blend of essential scented oils are mixed in solvents – typically alcohol and water. The life cycle of a fragrance is three-timed, starting with the top, moving onto the heart, and finally settling with the base notes.

When you spray on a perfume, you initially smell what is known as the ‘top note’. This is the lighter, airier smell which permeates through the air and hits you, and could last anywhere from 11 to 12 hours, depending on the concentration of your fragrance.
